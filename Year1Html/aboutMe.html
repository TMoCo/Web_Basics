Who am I?

You might have stumbled upon this website inadvertidly and may not know who I am. This is problematic as this website is entirely dedicated to my very self. Worry not as I am about to reveal my identity: My name is Thomas, or Tommy as I prefer to be nicknamed, Moreno-Cooper. The hyphen is very important. Born on the 30th of april 1999, I am the eldest son of a French engineer and a british English teacher. By the way, I'm fluent in French and Enlish.
