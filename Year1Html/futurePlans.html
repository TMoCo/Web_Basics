The future.

Well, here we are, the future. To be entirely honest, it isn't something I tend to focus on too much. I think that setting one's mind to something too early may deprive one of possibly better opportunities. Nevertheless, I would like to think that I may at some point work for a video game company one day. After all, it is one of the main reasons behind me taking a Computer Science degree.

One thing that I know I must do is visit some other countries. The farthest I have ever been from home was Denmark to visit one of my uncles. Hopefully I may one day be able to visit North and South America.

But before that...

I don't want to get ahead of myself so I'll think about the coming easter holidays. I'll be heading back down south to see my friends and family, I wasn't able to go down over the christmas break so I have been very much looking forward to it.

But most importantly, I'm going to see my little minx of a kitten Effie Gray, and give her a long awaited cuddle.
